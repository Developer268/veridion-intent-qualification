# Intent Qualification Queries
# Three target queries for the Veridion Data Challenge

Query 1: ev_battery_supply_chain
Description: Companies that are part of the electric vehicle battery supply chain.
This includes manufacturers of battery cells, modules, packs, cathode/anode materials,
electrolytes, separators, BMS systems, and related EV powertrain components.
Excludes: pure recyclers, lead-acid battery makers, alkaline battery companies.

Query 2: fintech_growth_proxy
Description: High-growth fintech companies with active funding signals.
Includes: neobanks, digital payment providers, lending platforms, insurtech, wealthtech,
regtech, and embedded finance companies that have raised or are raising venture capital.
Excludes: legacy/traditional banks, brick-and-mortar financial institutions.

Query 3: supplier_role_detection
Description: Companies acting as Tier-1 or Tier-2 suppliers/manufacturers/OEMs
in automotive, aerospace, industrial machinery, electronics, or semiconductor sectors.
Includes: component manufacturers, parts fabricators, OEM suppliers with quality certifications.
Excludes: pure retailers, end consumers, distributors with no manufacturing role.
